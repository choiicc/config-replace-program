Agency policy

Agent commission = net profit × commission ratio of the month.

The definition of net profit

Net Profit = Gross Membership of Offline Members - Total Membership Bonus of Offline Members - Total Deposit Fee for Offline Membership - (Total withdrawal amount of offline members × 2%).

Commission payment threshold

1. The effective membership number of the agents of this month must be ≥ 5 people before they reach the commission payment threshold.

2. Definition of valid members: Accumulative deposit in the current month reaches RMB300, or accumulative effective bets amount to RMB300. And each member must have an independent real name, user name, bank card, IP, etc., no multiple accounts or abnormal betting situation。

What to do if the minimum effective membership threshold is not reached

If there are less than 5 active members in the same month, the commissions will not be issued and will be accumulated。

The amount of commission to reach more than you can receive

Pay the minimum commission of IDR 100 IDR, such as commissions less than 100 IDR, then accumulate to 100 IDR before payment. All recommendations will be charged to management fees incurred in the course of each member and to the sales of direct members, which will be deducted directly from the monthly income of each referee。

Included in the monthly deduction of recommended income

1. Payments - Deposits and withdrawals involve all member referral fees。
2. Promotion Bonuses - Bonuses and rewards or rebates are given to referral members。

Commission calculation cycle and when paid

The commission calculation period is from the 1st to the 30th of each month. Settlement takes about 3 working days, the agency confirmed the commission amount is correct, you can submit my collection account. After the verification of the agency department, commission will arrive within 7 days。

Negative profit

If the agency's net profit for the month is negative, then the commission is negative for the month. The negative commissions will be deducted from the positive commissions accumulated next month. If the agency's net profit for the month is positive, but the number of active members off the assembly line does not meet the minimum standards, commissions will be accumulated. The total commission to be positive, and the effective membership of up to 5 months of the re-release.。

Other rules

1. The name of the agent must be the same as the name of the payee's bank account, otherwise the commission can not be paid。
2. If found to be malicious agents in any way to take commission, will not be paid the commission of the month。
3. If agents found not valid due to the number of members, repeated registration cobbler number, will not be paid the commission that month。
4. Any agents found to be offline, such as cheating, fraud or gambling, will be deducted from the effective number of members. If the agents are also involved, they will not be paid commissions for the current month。
5. Agents may not register multiple accounts, offenders will deduct agency commissions, serious cases will close its agent account, stop cooperation！
6. The same IP, the same name, the same account receivables member, can only be a member of offline agents. Agents themselves can not become their own offline members, and the same agent can only have an agent account。
