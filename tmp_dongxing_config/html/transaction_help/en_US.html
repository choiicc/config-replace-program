Deposit

1.How do I make a deposit into my account?

Click on my account then the recharge money button in the column on the left, you can then select your preferred payment method.

2.How long does it take for my deposit to get approved?

For most payment methods your deposit will be approved instantly.

3.Which deposit methods do you have available?

Please click Recharge in the top menu of any SevenBet.com page to see all of the payment methods available to you.

4.Will I be charged any fees when depositing into my SevenBet.com account?

There are no fees for depositing into your SevenBet.com account. However, kindly check your bank as it may incur some bank charges.

5.What is the minimum and maximum amounts that I can deposit at a time?

The minimum deposit amount is USD/EUR 10 and for the maximum amount which is accepted by i-Payments is USD/EUR 5000 or your currency equivalent per transaction.

6.How do I make a deposit from my bank account?

Please click ‘Recharge‘ in ‘My Account‘ and chose Bank transfers. You can activate this option by clicking ‘Deposit‘. Then follow the instructions on the screen.


Withdrawal

1.How can I make a withdrawal from my website account?

You can ask for a withdrawal at any time as long as you have met all requirements and have been authorized for withdrawals in our system. You can also withdraw as many times as you wish but, under anti money laundering and fraud prevention laws that SevenBet.com adhere to, we cannot allow withdrawals from your SevenBet.com account for 24 hours after a Deposit is made. All deposits must be wagered once prior to withdrawal. The minimum withdrawal amount is € 10.00.

2.Is there any limit that I can withdraw?

The minimum withdrawal is € 10.00 or equivalent to your currency. Maximum withdrawal is € 5000 per day or equivalent to your currency.

3.Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

You will need to contact SevenBet.com Customer Service and if the withdrawal has not been processed it can be stopped.

4.Can I get my winnings and deposit paid back to my credit/debit card?

Yes, you can as long as it is the same card that you used to make your original deposit and the card is able to accept a withdraw back onto it. If you are using i-Payments to make your original deposit, in this case, i-Payments doesn't allow a withdrawal in the form of a refund. It means that i-Payments cannot process any withdrawal requests from player by refunding back the money to the credit card used initially for deposit.

5.Why can’t I choose by which method I want to get paid?

This is due to Money laundering laws that we adhere to and also for yours and our security for all financial transactions, so unless authorized all refunds must go back to the same method as used for depositing.

6.Do you charge any withdrawal fees?

Withdrawal fees will vary depending on your chosen withdrawal method.

7.How long do withdrawals take to process?

This can vary depending on your chosen withdrawal method.

8.Can I make a withdrawal from my account and have it sent to someone else?

No, this is deemed as a third party transaction and will not be permitted in any circumstance.

9.What is SevenBet.com withdrawal policy?

Your account must be enabled and have the minimum amount of money required in order to withdraw. Some restrictions may apply to players participating in any SevenBet.com promotions.
